Services

What I Offer

I provide psychodynamic and relational therapy for adults and couples, both in-person in the Flatiron District and online throughout New York State.

01

Individual Psychoanalysis & Therapy

Individual therapy offers a dedicated space to explore your inner life, understand patterns that hold you back, and move toward deeper freedom and authenticity. We meet regularly — typically one to three times per week depending on your needs — to build a therapeutic relationship that becomes a resource for understanding and change.

This work can help with anxiety, depression, trauma, self-doubt, relationship difficulties, career stress, and the search for deeper meaning and fulfillment.

02

Couples Therapy

Couples therapy provides a space to improve communication, navigate conflict, and build a more connected, fulfilling relationship. Whether you are facing a specific crisis or a gradual sense of disconnection, we work together to understand the patterns that keep you stuck and to build new ways of relating.

I work with couples of all backgrounds and orientations, including those navigating issues of trust, intimacy, life transitions, and differing needs.

03

Consultation for Clinicians

I offer consultation and supervision for mental health professionals seeking to deepen their clinical work. Drawing on my psychoanalytic training and experience, I provide a collaborative space to think through complex cases, develop your clinical voice, and explore the dynamics of the therapeutic relationship.

04

Free 15-Minute Consultation

Starting therapy is a significant step, and it is important to find the right fit. I offer a free 15-minute phone consultation so we can discuss what you are looking for, answer your questions, and see if working together feels right.

There is no obligation to continue after this call. My goal is to help you make an informed decision about your care.

Approach

How I Work

Psychodynamic

We explore how past experiences and unconscious patterns shape your present life, relationships, and emotional world.

Relational

The therapeutic relationship itself becomes a tool for understanding how you connect with others and with yourself.

Trauma-Informed

We move at a pace that feels safe, with attention to how difficult experiences may continue to affect you.

Practical Details

Fees & Logistics

Fees

My standard fee is discussed during the initial consultation. I offer a sliding scale based on financial need. Please feel free to ask about this when we speak.

Insurance

I am an out-of-network provider. I can provide a monthly superbill that you may submit to your insurance company for reimbursement, depending on your plan.

Payment

I accept American Express, Cash, Check, Mastercard, PayPal, Venmo, Visa, and Zelle.

Location

My office is located at 156 5th Avenue, in the Flatiron District of Manhattan. I also offer sessions online via secure video for clients throughout New York State.

Session Length

Individual sessions are typically 45 minutes. Couples sessions and psychoanalytic sessions may be longer, depending on the frequency and nature of the work.
