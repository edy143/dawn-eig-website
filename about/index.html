Individual Psychoanalysis

An immersive, deeply collaborative process that uses the therapeutic relationship to explore your inner world in a sustained and meaningful way.

Couples Therapy

A space where the relationship itself becomes the focus, helping you understand dynamics, communicate more clearly, and feel more connected.

My Approach

My approach to therapy is primarily psychoanalytic. Rather than seeing symptoms as problems to eliminate, it understands them as expressions of underlying emotional conflicts, often outside of awareness.

Integrative & Flexible

While my training is grounded in psychoanalysis, I don't see therapy as one-size-fits-all. Each person brings different needs, goals, and ways of engaging in the process.

Psychoanalytic Training

I received my psychoanalytic training at the National Institute for the Psychotherapies, where I trained as a relationally oriented psychotherapist and psychoanalyst.

Education & Prior Career

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Psychology from the University of Colorado Boulder and later completed my MBA before transitioning into psychotherapy.

Location

My office is located at 156 5th Avenue. I am available to work by Zoom but find that working together in person is far preferable.

Therapy is a space for you to better understand yourself, work through challenges, and open new possibilities for how to live and connect with others. Together, we will explore patterns in your relationships, work, and inner life to uncover what may be holding you back from feeling more fulfilled and at ease.

In an earlier chapter of my life, I spent a decade working in finance. That experience helps me understand the pull between ambition, success, and personal well-being, and how those pressures affect daily life.

People I work with are often navigating the lasting impact of earlier experiences, along with present challenges such as relationship issues, career transitions, and a search for deeper fulfillment. My approach is psychodynamic and relational, grounded in how trauma and unconscious patterns shape emotional life.

Philosophy

My Approach

I work with people experiencing anxiety, depression, trauma, and self-doubt. My goal is to help you find insight, resilience, and the confidence to live with greater self-understanding and authenticity.

In this process, I aim to help you move toward a life of deeper freedom, emotional depth, and connection to what truly matters.
