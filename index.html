About

Understanding the space between ambition and well-being

Before becoming a psychoanalyst, I spent a decade working in finance. That experience gives me a particular understanding of the pull between ambition, success, and personal well-being — and how those pressures affect daily life.

Today, I help people find insight, resilience, and the confidence to live with greater self-understanding and authenticity.
